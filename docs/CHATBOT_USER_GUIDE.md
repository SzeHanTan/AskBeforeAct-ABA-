## 🎯 What to Ask

### 📚 Learning About Scams

**Good Questions:**
- "What is phishing and how does it work?"
- "How can I identify a romance scam?"
- "What are the warning signs of payment fraud?"
- "Tell me about job scams"
- "How do tech support scams operate?"

**Example Response:**
```
🤖 Phishing is a type of fraud where scammers pretend to be
   legitimate companies to steal your information.

   🚩 Warning Signs:
   1. Suspicious sender email
   2. Urgent language
   3. Requests for passwords
   4. Spelling errors
   5. Suspicious links

   ✅ Protection:
   - Verify sender carefully
   - Never click suspicious links
   - Contact company directly
   - Enable 2FA

   Would you like specific examples?
```

### 📰 Understanding News

**Good Questions:**
- "Summarize the latest scam news"
- "What are the current scam trends?"
- "Which scams are most common right now?"
- "What regions are affected by scams?"
- "Tell me about recent phishing attacks"

**Example Response:**
```
🤖 📊 Based on the latest news:

   **Key Trends:**
   1. Phishing attacks targeting banks (↑35%)
   2. Romance scams on dating apps
   3. Fake job offers promising easy money

   **Most Common:**
   - Phishing (45%)
   - Romance (25%)
   - Payment fraud (18%)

   **Affected Regions:**
   - Malaysia and Singapore
   - Chinese-speaking communities

   ⚠️ Be extra cautious of unsolicited banking
   messages and too-good-to-be-true job offers!
```

### 🔍 Analyzing Scenarios

**Good Questions:**
- "Is this a scam? [describe situation]"
- "Someone asked me to... is this normal?"
- "I received a message saying... should I trust it?"

**Example:**
```
You: "Someone on WhatsApp claims to be from my bank
     and asks for my credit card CVV. Is this a scam?"

🤖: 🚨 YES! This is definitely a scam!

    **Type:** Phishing / Banking Scam

    **Red Flags:**
    1. ❌ Contact via WhatsApp (banks don't do this)
    2. ❌ Asking for CVV (NEVER share this!)
    3. ❌ Unsolicited contact

    **What to Do:**
    1. ✅ DO NOT share any information
    2. ✅ Block the contact
    3. ✅ Call your bank using official number
    4. ✅ Report to Bank Negara: 1-300-88-5465

    Real banks NEVER ask for CVV, PIN, or OTP!
```

### 💡 Getting Tips

**Good Questions:**
- "How can I protect myself from phishing?"
- "Tips for safe online dating"
- "How to verify job offers"
- "Best practices for online payments"

**Example Response:**
```
🤖 Here are 5 tips to protect yourself from phishing:

   1. 🔍 Check sender email carefully
      Look for slight misspellings

   2. 🚫 Never click suspicious links
      Hover to see real URL first

   3. 📞 Contact directly
      Use official phone/website

   4. 🔐 Enable two-factor authentication
      Extra security layer

   5. 📧 Use email filters
      Let technology help you

   Remember: When in doubt, verify through
   official channels!
```

## ❓ FAQ

### Q: Is the chatbot free?
**A:** Yes! Completely free to use.

### Q: Are my conversations saved?
**A:** No, conversations are session-only and not stored.

### Q: Can the AI access my personal data?
**A:** No, the AI only sees what you type in chat.

### Q: How accurate is the AI?
**A:** Very accurate for general scam information, but always verify critical decisions independently.

### Q: Can I use it offline?
**A:** No, the chatbot requires internet to connect to Gemini AI.

### Q: What if the AI makes a mistake?
**A:** AI is a guide, not a guarantee. Always verify important information through official channels.

### Q: Can I share AI responses?
**A:** Yes, you can copy and share helpful information.
